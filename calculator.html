Compound Interest Calculator

How the Calculator Works

Enter your initial amount, monthly contribution, annual interest rate (as a percentage), and time period. The calculator uses the compound interest formula with monthly compounding to estimate how much your investment will grow.

Formula: FV = P(1 + r/n)^(nt) + PMT × [((1 + r/n)^(nt) - 1) / (r/n)]

Where P = Principal, r = Annual rate, n = Compounding periods per year, t = Time in years, PMT = Monthly payment
