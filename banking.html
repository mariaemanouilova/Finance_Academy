Banking

Explore types of bank accounts, how to choose the right provider, interest basics, and digital banking tools.

Types of Bank Accounts

  • Checking Accounts: Best for daily transactions and bill payments with debit card access
  • Savings Accounts: Earn interest on your deposits while maintaining easy access
  • Money Market Accounts: Higher interest rates for larger balances with check-writing privileges
  • Certificates of Deposit (CDs): Fixed-term savings with guaranteed interest rates

Choosing the Right Bank

When selecting a bank, consider:

  • Interest rates offered
  • Fees and charges
  • Digital banking features
  • Customer support availability
  • FDIC insurance protection

Digital Banking Tips

Modern banking has evolved to offer convenient digital solutions. Take advantage of mobile apps, online transfers, and automated bill payments to manage your finances efficiently and securely.
