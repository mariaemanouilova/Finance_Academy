Insurance

Understand life, health, property and liability insurance so you can select the right protection for your needs.

Why Insurance Matters

Insurance protects you and your family from financial loss due to unexpected events. Having the right coverage gives you peace of mind and safeguards your financial future.

Types of Insurance

  • Life Insurance: Provides financial security for dependents after death. Choose between term or whole life policies.
  • Health Insurance: Covers medical expenses including preventive care, treatment, and hospitalization.
  • Auto Insurance: Required by law in most places. Covers liability, collision, and comprehensive damages.
  • Homeowners Insurance: Protects your home and belongings from damage, theft, and liability claims.
  • Disability Insurance: Replaces income if you become unable to work due to injury or illness.

Choosing Coverage

Assess your needs based on your lifestyle, dependents, assets, and income. Don't over-insure, but ensure you have adequate protection. Review your policies annually as your circumstances change.

Understanding Premiums & Deductibles

Premiums are your regular payments. Deductibles are what you pay out-of-pocket before insurance kicks in. Higher deductibles usually mean lower premiums.
